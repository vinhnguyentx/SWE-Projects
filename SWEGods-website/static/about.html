About Page

Purpose

We are a group of talented computer science majors at the University of Texas at Austin taking the Software Engineering Course CS373 that is being taught by Professor Downing. The goal of this website is to present users who are interested in the Greek Mythology with interesting stories and facts to the beings who are related to the Greek Myth. Greek mythology is an interesting subject that started with the ancient Greeks around 2000BC and is still prevalent today when studying about the past Greek culture. Their stories has influenced the people of their time in a religious and cultural sense. We believe that Greek Mythology is interesting and want to create a website that will reflect our interest in it, and one in which a user could endlessly click on related information to learn more about the legends of the past.
